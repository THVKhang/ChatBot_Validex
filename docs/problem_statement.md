# Problem Statement

## Ten he thong
AI Blog Generator for Backoffice

## Mo ta ngan
He thong la mot chatbot noi bo ho tro nhan vien backoffice tao ban nhap blog bang prompt tu do. Nguoi dung khong can dien form co dinh ma chi can nhap yeu cau trong khung chat. He thong se phan tich yeu cau, truy xuat tai lieu lien quan tu knowledge base, va sinh noi dung phu hop.

## Muc tieu
- Ho tro tao blog nhanh hon
- Giam thoi gian viet nhap ban dau
- Tang tinh nhat quan ve noi dung
- Tan dung du lieu co san thay vi viet thu cong hoan toan

## Pham vi
Prototype doc lap trong pham vi thuc tap, khong chinh sua source code chinh cua he thong hien tai.

## Nguoi dung
- Nhan vien backoffice
- Nguoi phu trach noi dung/blog noi bo

## Input
Prompt text tu do tu nguoi dung.

Vi du:
- "Viet cho toi mot blog ve police check la gi"
- "Tao bai blog ngan gon cho employer"
- "Viet lai bai nay theo giong chuyen nghiep hon"

## Output
- Blog draft
- Co the kem outline
- Co the kem thong tin tham chieu da truy xuat
- Ho tro chinh sua tiep theo qua hoi thoai

## He thong lam gi
- Nhan prompt text tu do trong khung chat
- Parse yeu cau (topic, tone, audience, length)
- Retrieve tai lieu lien quan tu knowledge base mau
- Sinh outline va blog draft de backoffice chinh sua tiep

## He thong khong lam gi
- Khong sua source code chinh cua he thong main
- Khong auto publish bai viet len kenh production
- Khong xu ly van hanh production (phan quyen, monitor, SLA)
